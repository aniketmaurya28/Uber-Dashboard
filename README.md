# Uber-Dashboard 

Uber is one the leading travel booking application and websites active all over the world. This uber trip analysis dashboard analyzes the travel bookings of New York City which 
reveals day/night patterns, payment preferences, vehicle performance, and geographic hotspots for operational insights.

## Overview 

- Total Scale: 104K trips generating $1.6M revenue, averaging $14.98 fare, 3 miles distance, 16 min trips.

- Vehicles: UberX tops revenue ($1.7M) & distance (52 miles); UberXL, Black, Premium trail - dual bar charts.

- Donut chart: Uber Pay ( ~ 67), Cash ( ~32%) and rest (Amazon Pay and Google Pay).

- KPI's:
